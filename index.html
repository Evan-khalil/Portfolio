About Me

My name is Evan Khalil, I am 28 years old and live in Norrköping. I am a newly graduated systemdeveloper from Örebro University and am now ready to take the next step in my career and start working on what I am passionate about: developing systems from start to finish.
I am driven by the desire to streamline and systematize work and not leave anything by chance.
I am motivated by development, I thrive when the pace is high at work and I can work independently and carefully following instructions to ensure that we meet the quality standards set for the business.
